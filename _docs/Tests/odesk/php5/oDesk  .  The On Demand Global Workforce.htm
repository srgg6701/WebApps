Policy and Rules

Syllabus, Prerequisites and Instructions


Make sure to read the syllabus and prerequisites carefully before appearing for this test. Your test preparation should be based upon the syllabus given below.
Please carefully read the instructions as well and then click on the link at the bottom of this page to start the test. Your time will start only after you click on the link.

  • Advanced Concepts

Duration
  • 45 minutes.
No. of Questions
  • 40 multiple choice questions.
Instructions

  • The test consists of 40 questions to be completed in 45 minutes.

  • Each question has between 2 and 8 options, one or more may be correct.

  • There is no negative marking, therefore, make sure that you attempt all the questions.

  • The Timer Clock, timing your attempt is at the top center of the test window.

  • A link is displayed at the bottom right of the test window. You can click this link to provide specific feedback regarding a question. Please note that the Timer Clock will continue to run while you are typing out your feedback. Please ensure that your feedback is brief and specific.

  • A feedback form is provided at the end of the test so that you can convey your opinion about the test, and suggest improvements. The link for the feedback form will be displayed at the bottom of the test result page. The feedback form is optional.

  • This test is best viewed using Internet Explorer 5.0 or above or Mozilla Firefox 2.0 or above at 800x600 pixels resolution.

  • Two buttons are provided at the bottom of the test page - one for navigating to the next question and the other for navigating to the previous question. These are to be used for moving forwards and backwards within the test. Do not use the shortcut menu options (mouse right-click) or the keyboard for navigation.

  • Do not press the next button if you have not provided an answer to the question. Note that you will not be allowed to come back later and supply an answer.

  • You can use the back button to see the answers that you have provided to earlier questions, however, you will not be allowed to modify your earlier answers.

  • This test has an Auto-Resume Feature. In case, your Internet connection breaks and you are unable to complete the test in a single sitting, you can resume the test from exactly where you had left off by logging in to your oDesk account and once again clicking on the link to go to the test.

  • Click the Continue button only if you are sure that you want to start the test now.
